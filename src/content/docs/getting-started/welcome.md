ReleaseLoop is a music release management platform that helps labels, managers, and artists plan, execute, and track every aspect of a music release -- from initial setup through marketing and financial reporting.

## What you can do with ReleaseLoop

- **Plan releases** with tracks, tasks, and timelines
- **Coordinate marketing** across platforms with a built-in planner
- **Manage your artist roster** with Spotify-enriched profiles and public EPKs
- **Track financials** including budgets, royalty imports, and payout statements
- **Collaborate with your team** using shared workspaces, roles, and notifications
- **Maintain contacts** for PR, playlists, press, and more
- **Store and organize assets** with Google Drive integration

## How this knowledge base is organized

- **Getting Started** -- Setup your account and workspace

- **Financials** -- Budgets, royalties, and payouts
- **Artists** -- Artist profiles, Spotify sync, and EPKs

- **Templates & Calendar** -- Reusable workflows and scheduling

- **Billing & Plans** -- Subscription tiers and limits
