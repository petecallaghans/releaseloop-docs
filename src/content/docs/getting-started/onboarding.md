---
title: Onboarding Walkthrough
description: A step-by-step guide through the ReleaseLoop onboarding flow.
---

When you first sign up for ReleaseLoop, you'll be guided through a four-step onboarding flow to set up your workspace and create your first release.

## Step 1: Workspace setup

- Enter a name for your workspace (e.g., your label name)
- Select your role from the options: label, artist manager, artist, distributor, PR/marketing, or other

Your workspace is the shared space where all your releases, artists, and team members live.

## Step 2: Add your first artist

You have two options:

- **Search Spotify** -- Type an artist name to find them on Spotify. Selecting an artist will auto-populate their profile photo, follower count, and popularity score.
- **Create manually** -- Enter the artist name and details by hand.

You can always add more artists later from the Artists page.

## Step 3: Create your first release

Fill in the basics:

- **Title** -- The release name
- **Artists** -- Select from artists you've added
- **Release date** -- When the music goes live
- **Type** -- Single, EP, or Album

## Step 4: Plan marketing

Add initial marketing activities to your release:

- Choose platforms (Instagram, TikTok, Spotify, etc.)
- Set the phase (Pre-save, Release Week, Post-Release)
- Add descriptions for each activity

## After onboarding

Once complete, you'll land on your **Dashboard** where you'll see:

- Your release statistics
- Upcoming releases
- An onboarding checklist tracking your progress
- Quick action buttons

The onboarding checklist on the dashboard tracks additional setup steps like adding team members. It disappears once all steps are complete.
