---

This guide walks you through the essentials to start managing releases right away.

## 1. Create your account

Sign up at [app.releaseloop.com](https://app.releaseloop.com) using your email or sign in with Google or GitHub. After verifying your email, you'll be taken through the onboarding flow.

## 2. Set up your workspace

During onboarding, you'll:

1. **Name your workspace** -- this is your team's shared space (e.g., your label or management company name)
2. **Select your role** -- label, artist manager, artist, distributor, PR/marketing, or other
3. **Add your first artist** -- search Spotify to auto-populate or create manually
4. **Create your first release** -- set the title, artists, release date, and type

## 3. Build out your release

Once your release is created, use the tabs on the release detail page:

![Release detail page showing tabs for Tracks, Tasks, Assets, Discussion, Financials, Marketing, and Activity](../../../assets/screenshots/release-detail.png)

| Tab | What it does |
|-----|-------------|
| **Tracks** | Add songs with ISRC codes and artist credits |
| **Tasks** | Create a checklist of things to do before release |
| **Financials** | Set a budget and track expenses |
| **Marketing** | Plan social posts, playlist pitches, and PR activities |
| **Assets** | Upload artwork and connect Google Drive |
| **Comments** | Discuss the release with your team |

## 4. Invite your team

Go to **Settings > Team Members** and invite collaborators by email. Assign roles to control what each person can access.

## 5. Explore more features

- Use **Templates** to save and reuse task checklists
- Check the **Calendar** for a timeline view of all releases
- Import **Royalty CSVs** to track revenue by release
- Build **EPKs** as public profiles for your artists

For a detailed walkthrough of the onboarding flow, see [Onboarding Walkthrough](/getting-started/onboarding/).
