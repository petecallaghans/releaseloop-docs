title: Notifications
description: How to configure and manage notifications in ReleaseLoop.
---

ReleaseLoop sends notifications to keep your team informed about task assignments, deadlines, and updates.

## Notification types

| Notification | When it triggers |
|-------------|-----------------|
| **Task assigned** | When a task is assigned to you |
| **Task due soon** | 24 hours before a task's due date |
| **Task overdue** | When a task passes its due date without being completed |

## In-app notifications

Click the **bell icon** in the top navigation to view your notifications. New notifications appear as a badge count on the bell.

From the notification panel you can:

- View all recent notifications
- Click a notification to navigate to the relevant item
- Mark notifications as read

## Email notifications

Important notifications are also sent via email. This ensures you don't miss critical deadlines even when you're not actively using ReleaseLoop.

## Configuring preferences

1. Go to **Settings > Notifications**
2. Toggle each notification type on or off

By default, notifications are turned off. Enable the ones that matter to your workflow.

## Tips

- Enable "Task due soon" notifications to get a 24-hour heads up on deadlines
- If you're a workspace admin managing multiple team members, enable assignment notifications so people know when they have new work
