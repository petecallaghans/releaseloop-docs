title: Marketing Planner
description: How to plan and track marketing activities for your releases.
---

The **Marketing** tab on a release helps you plan and coordinate all promotional activities across platforms.

## Adding marketing activities

1. Open a release and go to the **Marketing** tab
2. Click **Add Activity**
3. Fill in:
   - **Title** -- description of the activity
   - **Platform** -- where it will happen (Instagram, TikTok, Spotify, YouTube, Email, Press, etc.)
   - **Phase** -- when in the release cycle: Pre-save, Release Week, or Post-Release
   - **Status** -- Draft, Scheduled, Published, or Completed
   - **Date** -- when the activity is planned

## Release phases

Marketing activities are organized into three phases:

| Phase | When | Example activities |
|-------|------|-------------------|
| **Pre-save** | Before release day | Pre-save links, teaser posts, playlist pitching |
| **Release Week** | The week of release | Launch posts, press releases, email blasts |
| **Post-Release** | After release day | Performance posts, remixes, playlist follow-ups |

## Platforms

ReleaseLoop supports tracking activities across all major platforms:

- Instagram
- TikTok
- Spotify
- YouTube
- X (Twitter)
- Facebook
- Email
- Press / PR
- Other

## Using with templates

You can include marketing activities in [release templates](/templates/release-templates/) to reuse your promotional playbook across releases. When you apply a template, both tasks and marketing activities are imported.

## Calendar integration

All marketing activities with dates appear on the [Calendar](/templates/calendar/) view, giving you a timeline of promotional efforts across all releases.
