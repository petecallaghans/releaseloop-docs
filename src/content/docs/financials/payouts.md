---

Payout statements let you calculate and track what you owe to artists based on imported royalty data.

:::note
Payout statements require a **Label** plan.
:::

## Overview

The Payouts section (within Royalties) lets you:

- View summary cards for total royalties and payout status
- Generate payout statements for specific periods
- Track historical payouts

## Generating a payout statement

1. Go to **Royalties > Payouts**
2. Click **Generate Statement**
3. Select the period and artists to include
4. Review the calculated amounts
5. Confirm to create the statement

Payout amounts are calculated from the royalty data you've imported via CSV.

## Payout history

All generated statements are saved and accessible from the Payouts tab. Each statement shows:

- Period covered
- Artists included
- Total amount
- Statement date

## Prerequisites

For accurate payouts, make sure you have:

- [Imported royalty CSVs](/financials/royalty-imports/) for the relevant period
- Tracks with correct ISRCs linked to releases
- Releases with correct UPCs or catalogue numbers
