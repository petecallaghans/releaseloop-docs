---
title: Budgets & Revenue
description: How to track budgets, expenses, and revenue for your releases.
---

The **Financials** tab on a release lets you track the money side of your releases -- what you're spending and what you're earning.

## Setting a budget

1. Open a release and go to the **Financials** tab
2. Set an overall budget amount for the release
3. Your workspace's default currency is used (configurable in Settings > Workspace)

## Tracking expenses

Add individual expenses to track where your budget is going:

- **Description** -- what the expense is for
- **Amount** -- how much it costs
- **Category** -- type of expense (e.g., production, marketing, distribution)
- **Payment status** -- Planned, Pending, or Paid

The financials view shows a running total of expenses against your budget so you can see how much remains.

## Revenue tracking

Track revenue coming in from different sources:

- **Manual revenue entries** -- add revenue from platforms or sales
- **Royalty imports** -- automatically populated when you [import distributor CSVs](/financials/royalty-imports/)

The combined view shows both manual and royalty-sourced revenue, giving you a complete picture of a release's financial performance.

## Royalty breakdown

If you've imported royalty data, the Financials tab also shows:

- Revenue broken down by source/distributor
- Per-track earnings (matched via ISRC)
- Total royalties for the release

See [Royalty Imports](/financials/royalty-imports/) for details on importing distributor data.
