---
title: Tracking Interactions
description: How to log and track interactions with your contacts.
---

Each contact in ReleaseLoop has an interaction timeline so you can track your communication history.

## Logging an interaction

1. Open a contact's detail page
2. Scroll to the **Interactions** section
3. Click **Add Interaction**
4. Enter the details of the interaction (what was discussed, outcome, next steps)
5. The interaction is timestamped and added to the timeline

## Interaction timeline

The timeline shows all logged interactions in chronological order, giving you a complete history of your relationship with the contact. This helps you:

- Remember what was discussed last time
- Track follow-up commitments
- See the progression of the relationship

## Success tracking

ReleaseLoop tracks the success rate for each contact:

- **Successful outcomes** -- when outreach leads to a positive result (e.g., playlist placement, press coverage)
- **Pending outcomes** -- still waiting for a response or decision

This helps you identify your most valuable contacts and focus your outreach efforts.

## Notes

Each contact also has a free-form notes field for internal comments that don't fit into the interaction timeline -- things like preferences, availability, or context about how you connected.
