title: Managing Contacts
description: How to add and organize your industry contacts in ReleaseLoop.
---

The Contacts page is your CRM for music industry relationships -- playlist curators, bloggers, PR agents, influencers, and more.

## Adding a contact

1. Go to the **Contacts** page
2. Click **Add Contact**
3. Fill in their details:
   - **Name** -- contact's full name
   - **Email** -- primary email address
   - **Phone** -- phone number (optional)
   - **Company** -- their organization (optional)
   - **Website** -- their website or profile link (optional)
   - **Social links** -- social media profiles
   - **Category** -- type of contact (see below)
   - **Genres** -- music genres they cover or are interested in

## Contact categories

Organize contacts by their role in the industry:

- Playlist curator
- Blogger / Writer
- PR / Publicist
- Influencer
- Radio
- Promoter
- Other

## Relationship status

Track where each contact stands in your outreach:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Outreach** | Initial contact, not yet responded |
| **Contacted** | You've reached out |
| **Interested** | They've shown interest |
| **Not Interested** | They've declined |

## Searching and filtering

The contacts list supports:

- **Search** by name, email, or company
- **Filter by category** -- show only curators, bloggers, etc.
- **Filter by relationship status** -- focus on active leads
- **Filter by genre** -- find contacts relevant to a specific release

## Linking contacts to releases

Contacts can be linked to specific releases, which is useful for tracking which PR contacts or curators are associated with each campaign.
