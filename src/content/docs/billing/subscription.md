---
title: Managing Your Subscription
description: How to upgrade, downgrade, and manage your ReleaseLoop subscription.
---

Subscription management is handled through the **Billing** page, accessible from the sidebar.

## Viewing your current plan

The Billing page shows:

- Your current plan name and status
- Current storage usage
- Feature limits for your plan
- Options to upgrade

## Upgrading your plan

1. Go to the **Billing** page
2. Compare plans and click **Upgrade** on the tier you want
3. You'll be redirected to Stripe's secure checkout
4. Complete the payment
5. Your workspace is upgraded immediately

## Managing your subscription

Click **Manage Subscription** on the Billing page to open the Stripe customer portal where you can:

- Update your payment method
- Switch between monthly and annual billing
- View invoices and payment history
- Cancel your subscription

## Who can manage billing

Only workspace **Owners** and **Admins** can access the Billing page and manage subscriptions.

## What happens when you hit a limit

If you reach a plan limit (e.g., maximum number of releases or artists), you'll see a message prompting you to upgrade. Existing data is never deleted -- you just won't be able to create new items beyond the limit.

## Cancellation

If you cancel your subscription:

- You retain access until the end of your current billing period
- Your data is preserved
- You can resubscribe at any time to regain full access
