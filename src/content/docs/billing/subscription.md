---
title: Managing Your Subscription
description: Upgrade, downgrade, update payment details, and manage your ReleaseLoop subscription through Stripe.
---

Subscription management is handled through the **Billing** page, accessible from the sidebar.

## Viewing your current plan

The Billing page shows:

- Your current plan name and status
- Current storage usage
- Feature limits for your plan (releases, artists, contacts, EPKs)
- Options to upgrade

## Upgrading your plan

When your operation grows -- you sign more artists, bring on team members, or need royalty imports and templates -- upgrading takes about 30 seconds:

1. Go to the **Billing** page
2. Compare plans and click **Upgrade** on the tier you want
3. You are redirected to Stripe's secure checkout
4. Complete the payment
5. Your workspace is upgraded immediately -- new features and higher limits are available right away

## Managing your subscription

Click **Manage Subscription** on the Billing page to open the Stripe customer portal where you can:

- Update your payment method
- Switch between monthly and annual billing (annual saves you roughly 2 months)
- View invoices and payment history
- Cancel your subscription

## Who can manage billing

Only workspace **Owners** and **Admins** can access the Billing page and manage subscriptions. This means your A&R and marketing team members will not accidentally change your plan or see payment details.

## What happens when you hit a limit

If you reach a plan limit -- for example, you have 15 releases on the Solo plan and try to create a 16th -- you will see a message prompting you to upgrade. Your existing data is never deleted or locked. You just cannot create new items beyond the limit until you move to a higher tier.

## Cancellation

If you cancel your subscription:

- You retain access until the end of your current billing period
- Your data is preserved -- release metadata, artist profiles, contacts, and financials all stay intact
- You can resubscribe at any time to pick up right where you left off
